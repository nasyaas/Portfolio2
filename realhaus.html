| The Project |


This project was a redesign for a Real Estate investment website. The original website contained a great deal of text and information and many sponsors had provided feedback that the previous colorway chosen made it difficult to read. Going into the redesign I put a focus on clarity and organization. Information was redistributed into bite-sized chuncks making it easier to read and not so overwhelming so that investors and sponsors alike would be able to find the information they need quickly and with ease. (All names and information have been changed to maintain the privacy of the company.)
